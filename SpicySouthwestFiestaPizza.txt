Title: Spicy Southwest Fiesta Pizza

Ingredients:
(its a disgrace)

1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
1 cup black beans, cooked and drained
1/2 cup corn kernels
1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
1 cup pepper jack cheese, shredded
1/4 cup pickled jalape'f1os, sliced
1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
Avocado slices for topping
Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:
Preheat your oven according to the pizza dough instructions.
Roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness.
Spread black beans, corn, and red bell pepper evenly over the pizza dough.
Sprinkle shredded pepper jack cheese on top of the vegetables.
Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
Remove from the oven and scatter pickled jalape'f1os and chopped cilantro over the pizza.
Top with slices of fresh avocado.
Serve with lime wedges for squeezing over the pizza before enjoying.

Experience the bold and spicy flavors of the Southwest in every bite!
