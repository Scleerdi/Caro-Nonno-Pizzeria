Title: Savory Mediterranean Garden Pizza

Ingredients:

(oooohhh)

1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, rehydrated and sliced
1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
1/2 cup artichoke hearts, quartered
1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted
Fresh oregano leaves
Olive oil for drizzling

Instructions:
Preheat your oven according to the pizza dough instructions.
Roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness.
Spread sun-dried tomatoes, Kalamata olives, and artichoke hearts evenly over the pizza dough.
Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese on top of the vegetables.
Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
Remove from the oven and sprinkle toasted pine nuts and fresh oregano leaves over the pizza.
Drizzle olive oil over the top before serving.

Enjoy the rich and bold flavors inspired by the Mediterranean!}
